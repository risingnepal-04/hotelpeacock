    Hotel Peacock -family running hotel

    Boasting a garden, Hotel Peacock - Family Running Hotel is set in Sauraha and also provides a terrace and a bar. This property is located a short distance from attractions such as Chitwan National Park. The hotel offers a Typical Nepali restaurant. The rooms in the hotel are fitted with a TV, fan and air-conditioning. Rooms are complete with a private bathroom while selected rooms will provide you with a balcony. Some guest rooms include a seating area, electric kettle and refrigerator.

    • Guests can enjoy local Tharu style meals at the property.
    • The area is popular for cycling, and bike hire is available at the accommodation.
    • Guests can enjoy safari activities at Chitwan National Park.
